Objectives


The objectives of this study are aligned directly with the general and specific problems
above so that every development target has explicit operational and literature-based
justification. Each objective is formulated as a direct development response to a
corresponding problem condition validated by interviews and supported by recent RRL.

General Objective:

To develop MarriottConnect as an integrated student information system with decision
support analytics that centralizes registrar, finance, academic, and stakeholder workflows
to deliver timely, reliable, and role-governed decisions for Marriott School.

This general objective is relevant because Marriott School requires a single operational
platform that can convert fragmented transactions into coordinated institutional outputs.
According to Zulueta et al. (2021), MIS effectiveness depends on process alignment as
much as software capability, while Kanona (2022) shows that centralized data structures
improve consistency and control. Therefore, the general objective addresses both day-to-
day transaction efficiency and leadership-level decision support.

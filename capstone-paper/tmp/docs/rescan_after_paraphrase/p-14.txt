General Objective:

To develop MarriottConnect as an integrated student information system with decision
support analytics for Marriott School.

This general objective is relevant because Marriott School requires a single operational
platform that can convert fragmented transactions into coordinated institutional outputs.

according to Zulueta et al., (2021). Kanona (2022) has found that centralized data structures
result in better consistency and therefore provide better control. Thus, the overall objective
includes efficiency of everyday transactions, as well as decision-making at the leadership
level.

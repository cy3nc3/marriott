Statement of the Problem

This study defines the operational problems of Marriott School in terms of cross-office
data continuity, transaction reliability, academic coordination, stakeholder transparency,
and institutional decision support. According to Zulueta et al. (2021), MIS success depends
on both technical fit and process standardization; therefore, problem identification must
capture workflow behavior across offices, not only software feature gaps. The appendices
and recent literature consistently show that fragmentation is not a single-module issue but
a system-wide coordination condition that affects registrar, finance, teacher, parent, and
administrative outcomes.

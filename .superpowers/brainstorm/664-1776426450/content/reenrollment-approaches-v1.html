Returning Student Re-enrollment: Approach Options

Goal: LRN-only start, editable prefill, ongoing school year only, and grade auto-promotion (+1).

A

Inline Expand (Recommended)

Step 1 starts with only LRN. After 12 digits + blur/Enter, show loader then expand Step 1 in place with student fields prefilled when matched.

Pros

  • Fastest for registrar; minimal cognitive switch
  • Keeps existing wizard structure mostly intact
  • Easy status: New vs Returning

Cons

  • Needs careful Step 1 state handling
B

Split Screen: Lookup Then Wizard

Separate LRN lookup card before wizard. Clicking Continue opens full wizard prefilled and promotion-aware.

Pros

  • Clear separation of concerns
  • Simpler component logic

Cons

  • Extra click and visual jump
  • Feels less instant
C

Modal-Based LRN Detection

LRN field triggers summary modal on match; registrar confirms then wizard gets prefilled.

Pros

  • Explicit confirmation reduces wrong-match risk

Cons

  • Interruptive for high-volume use
  • More UI complexity

Recommendation: A (Inline Expand).

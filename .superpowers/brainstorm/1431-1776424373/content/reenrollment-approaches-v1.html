Returning Student Re-enrollment: Approach Options

Goal: LRN-only start, editable prefill, ongoing school year only, and grade auto-promotion (+1).

A

Inline Expand (Recommended)

Step 1 starts with only LRN. After 12 digits + blur/Enter, show loader then expand Step 1 in place with student fields prefilled when matched.

Pros

  • Fastest for registrar; minimal cognitive switch
  • Keeps existing wizard structure mostly intact
  • Easy to communicate status: New vs Returning

Cons

  • Needs careful UI state handling in Step 1
B

Split Screen: Lookup Then Wizard

Separate LRN lookup card before wizard. Clicking "Continue" opens full wizard already prefilled and promotion-aware.

Pros

  • Clear separation of concerns
  • Simpler component logic than deep inline states

Cons

  • One extra click and visual jump
  • Feels less "instant"
C

Modal-Based LRN Detection

LRN field opens a modal on match with student summary + "Use Data". Wizard gets prefilled after confirmation.

Pros

  • Strong explicit confirmation
  • Lower risk of accidental wrong match usage

Cons

  • Interruptive and slower for frequent use
  • More UI complexity to maintain

Recommendation: A (Inline Expand), because it best supports registrar speed while preserving your existing wizard UX and backend behavior.
